How AudioGrab Works - Simple Recording Process

AudioGrab operates entirely offline. Your microphone access is only used for recording, and all audio data stays on your device. No internet connection required.

Step 1: Grant Microphone Permission

When you first use AudioGrab, your browser will ask for microphone permission. This is required for audio recording functionality.

Step 2: Start Audio Recording

Click the red recording button to begin capturing high-quality audio from your microphone.

Step 3: Stop Recording and Save File

Click the button again to stop recording. Your audio file will be saved to your device's download folder with smart naming.
