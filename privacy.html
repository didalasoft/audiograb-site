Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 30, 2025

Introduction

DidalaSoft ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AudioGrab application.

Important: AudioGrab works offline for core functionality. We collect minimal usage analytics to improve the app experience.

Data Collection

We collect minimal usage data to improve the app:

  • Feature usage: Which features you use most often
  • App performance: Crash reports and performance metrics
  • Device info: Device type and app version (no personal identifiers)

What We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Audio recordings or file contents
  • Location data
  • Contact information

Audio Recording

AudioGrab requests microphone permission to record audio locally on your device. All recordings are stored only on your device and are never transmitted anywhere.

File Storage

The app accesses your device's storage to save and load audio recordings. These files remain on your device and are not shared with us or any third parties.

Offline Operation

AudioGrab's core recording functionality works entirely offline. Analytics data is sent only when you have an internet connection.

Analytics Services

We use analytics services to understand app usage patterns and improve performance. This helps us:

  • Identify which features are most useful
  • Fix bugs and improve stability
  • Test new features with gradual rollouts

Your Rights

Your audio captures remain completely private and under your control. Since we only collect minimal usage analytics, you can limit data collection by using the app offline.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the app store.
